> AI engineering leader with **a decade of experience** designing and delivering machine-learning systems end to end — from data pipelines and feature engineering through model development, deployment, and monitoring — across both the **public and private sectors**.

- **Currently** leading the **AI &amp; data initiatives of the Mexican Senate's Acquisitions Department** — a full-stack platform that embeds ML and automated, auditable workflows into the procurement lifecycle.
- **Stanford-certified in Artificial Intelligence** (XCS221) and pursuing an **M.Eng. in AI Engineering** *(exp. 2027)*.
